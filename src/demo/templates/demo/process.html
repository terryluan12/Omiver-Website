Sample Collection

Omiver’s sample collection kit will include a dry-blood spot kit. This kit will include all the materials and packaging for adequate whole-blood sample collection.

Sample Shipment

The collection kit will include labels designed for shipment to our laboratory facility. Once the client has collected their sample, they can simply reattach the label to the packaging and place it in the mail!

Biomarker Detection

After our laboratory receives the client’s samples, our panel will detect and analyze 100+ crucial biomarkers that impact their diet, fitness, and performance goals.

Data Analysis

We then input the client’s biomarker test results and profile into our proprietary model to generate personalized fitness regimens and diet plans. These recommendations will be based on the client’s unique performance goals and needs.

Quality Control

Our in-house experts will review the plans and verify the quality and accuracy of our recommendations to ensure they cater to your client’s needs.

Platform Reporting

Our platform will include full details of all your clients for easy monitoring and feedback reporting.
